Social media for teachers and schools

Master social media - for your school, your staff, your parents and pupils

We offer three tailored training programmes designed to meet the needs of your school.

For schools:

promote and protect

Learn the art of strategic social media promotion to showcase your school’s achievements, events, and unique offerings while reinforcing and protecting its reputation. Attract prospective students and foster a sense of pride within the community.

For parents:

secure the digital playground

Equip parents with the knowledge to protect their children’s online safety. Our specialised training covers essential topics such as social media safety, cyberbullying prevention, and guidance on fostering a healthy digital environment at home. Empower parents to become digital guardians.

For staff:

safeguard your school's reputation

Arm your staff with the tools they need to navigate the digital landscape responsibly. Our training provides comprehensive policies and guidance, ensuring every staff member understands their role in protecting the school’s reputation. Foster a culture of responsibility and accountability.

Why Social Elements for your school social media training?

At Social Elements, we pride ourselves on delivering top-notch social media training. Our team comprises seasoned social media strategists and trainers each with a wealth of knowledge and practical experience. We understand that your organisation is unique, with distinct needs and objectives. That’s why our approach is tailored to meet your bespoke requirements.
